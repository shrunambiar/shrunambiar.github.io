      Product Description
      Featuring a powerful 5000 mAh battery, the Zenfone Max is a budget phone that's the answer to all your battery woes. This phone's perfect for your everyday needs such as phone calls, texts, managing social media, browsing the Web or streaming YouTube videos.
      Battery

      With a battery capacity of 5000 mAh, you can be on this phone 24/7. Play games, watch videos, browse the Web or switch between Facebook, Twitter, and Instagram. Enjoy a standby time of up to 38 days, 3G talk time of up to 37.6 hours, Wi-Fi Web browsing of up to 32.5 hours and a video playback time of up to 22.6 hours.

      5000 mAh
      Power Bank Function

      Are you trying to transfer pictures from your friend's phone that's about to die? Use the Zenfone Max to charge her phone using an OTG adapter.

      Design

      This phone features a curvaceous frame and a metal-effect bezel. With the look-and-feel of embossed leather, this phone's luxurious etching on the rear improves grip. The slim profile makes it easy to slip this phone into your pant pocket.

      Slim Profile
      Display

      This phone has a 13.97 cm (5.5) HD IPS display with 720 x 1280 pixels resolution.

      Operating System

      Now you can access the apps in a jiffy as this phone supports touch gestures. The Android 6.0.1-based Zen UI is simple, intuitive and easy to personalise. You can also easily manage your pictures with Creative Studio.

      6.0.1-based Zen UI
      Performance

      Thanks to Adreno 306 GPU, you can kill time while you're on your way home by playing Candy Crush Saga or Subway Surfers. Now you can switch between apps or browse the Web without any lag as this phone has 1.5 GHz octa-core Qualcomm Snapdragon 615 processor and 2 GB RAM.

      Say no to blurry pictures. Featuring a dual-LED flash, this phone's 13 MP rear camera features fast laser auto-focus for capturing clear pictures.
