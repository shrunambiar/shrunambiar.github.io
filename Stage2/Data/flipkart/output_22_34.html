      • Product Description
      Carefully polished with 15 layers of craftsmanship, this Honor phone comes with a 3D grating effect that provides the phone an elegance that you are going to love. With an ultra narrow bezel, the phone feels nice and comfortable when you hold it.
      12 MP Dual Lenses

      The phone has dual 12MP lenses with features like wide aperture mode and professional mode to help you take pictures and record videos in a creative way. The phone also comes with a built-in ISP that is aligned with the depth measurement ISP that helps you take pictures with better focus and speed. With hybrid auto-focus, precise depth focus and contrast focus, you can take high quality photos in almost any lighting condition. With a monochrome sensor, 1.25mm pixels and an RGB, the phone’s camera can capture more light to help you take high quality pictures that are bright and crisp.

      Full HD Display

      This Honor phone comes with an FHD display and has 96 percent high color gamut, dynamic pixel level contrast adjustment technology and a color enhancement feature making pictures brighter and more visible on your screen. The phone also has an eye care mode that filters out part of blue light, thus making images safe and comfortable on the eyes.

      Intelligent Communication

      With the Honor 8 phone, you can always enjoy the best possible reception, thanks to the double antenna that seamlessly switches to the one with the stronger connection. You can even quickly register on local networks while you are on roaming, thanks to Huawei’s international carrier database.

      Audio And Entertainment

      The phone has an integrated speaker unit to let you enjoy high-quality music playback. With the dynamic noise cancelling and a high sensitive microphone, you can enjoy great handsfree support. Features like volume amplification and voice+ technology ensure that you enjoy great audio quality. With Smart Control 4.0, you can control devices even without an Internet connection.

      Battery Features

      This phone has a great battery capacity of 3000mAh that can be maximized with the SmartPower 4.0, giving you a normal usage of 1.77 days and a heavy usage of 1.22 days. You can even watch videos offline with the ten hour offline feature. With the 9V/2A fast charging technology, you can charge your phone for 30 minutes to enjoy 47 percent of charge. You can also enjoy a two hour call or listen to music offline for six hours with just ten minutes of charge.

      A Smooth User Experience

      This phone comes with a 2.3GHz Octa Core processor and 4GB RAM that gives you great speed to make your games more enjoyable and multitasking easy. With an i5 co-processor aligned with the Kirin 950 SoC chipset, you have better processing speed, response time and a great battery life.

      Front Facing Camera

      The phone’s front facing camera comes with 8MP giving all your selfies a superior quality, even those taken in low light.

      The Touch And Feel

      This phone unlocks in 0.4 seconds. With 3D fingerprint reading and level 4 fingerprint recognition algorithm, the phone helps you keep all your data secure. The phone also comes with an auto learning skills set and a built-in custom smart key function for speed and a sense of style. You can also enjoy greater accessibility to the Internet with features like NFC tag read N write, P2P transfer, SIM/ HCE-based mobile payment.
