      Product Description
      Do more with the ASUS ZenFone Max. It has a mammoth-sized battery that boasts a long standby time and the ability to recharge compatible devices. Watch movies, take care of work or take a moment to capture an inspiring landscape for Instagram.
      Battery

      The phone packs a long-lasting 5000 mAh lithium-polymer battery. It boasts a standby time of 38.1 days. With up to 37.6 hours of talktime on 3G or 32.5 hours of Wi-Fi web browsing, the device has enough juice to keep up with any power user. The ZenFone Max can even be used to charge other compatible devices.

      Your Go-Everywhere Power Bank

      Thanks to the 5000 mAh battery, this phone can be used as a power bank to charge other devices. Just connect the device to the Zenfone Max using a USB on-the-go (OTG) cable.

      13 MP PixelMaster Camera

      The 13 MP PixelMaster camera has a f/2.0-aperture lens for high-resolution photos captured without shutter lag. Industry-leading Low Light mode uses pixel-merging technology to ensure up to 400% brighter photos at night or in low-light. The ultra-fast, laser auto-focus feature greatly reduces blurring and enhances image stabilization.

      DSLR-like Camera Controls

      The camera has an intuitive Full Manual mode. Adjust all the settings of the camera manually, as you would with a DSLR, and capture your loved one's birthday. All 13 professional settings and controls are easy to access while framing a shot. With the 85-degree field of view, the phone is perfect to capture large group photos at picturesque locations.

      Performance

      The ZenFone Max is powered by a quad-core, Qualcomm® Snapdragon™ 410 processor that clocks at 1 GHz. With 2 GB LPDDR3 RAM, the processor pushes multitasking further, allowing users to play 3D games or edit HD videos seamlessly. The device features LTE Category 4 for download speeds of up to 150Mbps and Dual SIM standby.

      Display

      The ZenFone Max has a 13.97 cm (5.5) HD IPS display and with a resolution of 1280x720, it offers wide, 178-degree viewing angles, with a captivating 267 ppi pixel density.

      Storage

      The new ZenUI, made especially for ASUS phones, has more than 1000 features and additions to the stock Android interface. The UI also features customizable gestures, such as double tap to wake up. With customizable themes and a new layout, the UI expands white space and places the information you need intuitively.
