      Product Description
      Meet the Ivoomi Me3 - the smartphone that takes your mobile experience to the next level. Powered by a quad-core MTK 6737 64-bit processor and 2 GB of DDR3 RAM, along with a 3000 mAh battery, this smartphone ensures a powerful performance.
      Crystal-clear and Super Bright Display

      Its 13.20 cm (5.2) screen with a 2.5D curved glass display features narrow bezels and full lamination display and emits 460 nits brightness to provide you with an immersive phone-viewing experience.

      8 MP Front Camera

      Its front camera, with a 1.12um sensor, an aperture ratio of f/2.0 and 76-degree wide-angle lens, helps click beautiful and gorgeous selfies. The camera’s Beauty Mode and front LED Flash further gives you the ability to click selfies that are bright and in focus.

      8 MP Rear Camera

      The rear camera comes with a 1.0 um Samsung sensor and an aperture ratio of f/2.0, along with a LED flash, Auto Focus, Auto HDR Mode, Low light Photography, and Beauty & Panorama mode, which allow you to capture life’s best moments with every detail in focus.

      3000 mAh Battery and Fast charging 2.0

      The powerful battery greatly enhances system speed and offers rapid charging to keep you connected all day long.

      16 GB Internal Memory

      Store your heart’s content on this smartphone without worrying about running out of storage space. The Me3’s memory can also be expandable by up to 128 GB with a microSD card.

      Powerful Processor

      Powered by a quad-core MTK 6737 64-bit processor and 2 GB DDR3 RAM, this smartphone ensures a glitch-free multitasking experience.

      Android 7.0

      Enjoy Android’s new and advanced features at your fingertips.

      4G VoLTE

      This Dual SIM smartphone offers a superior connectivity, a high-speed HD video calling, and an improved voice quality.
